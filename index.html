ABOUT THE FLAGSHIP EVENT OF ‘DIU JOB UTSOB-2022’

With a tremendously successful track record of ‘On-Campus Spot Recruitment’ events, it is our privilege hosting ‘DIU job Utsob’ for the year 2022.

DIU Job Utsob-2022 is the annual ‘Big bang career event’ held by the Career Development Center (CDC) of Daffodil International University. The program aims to provide a platform for DIU graduates to connect with the leading and constantly expanding companies of their preferences, and for the corporate sector- an opportunity to recruit interns and employees possessing a suitable career profile.

This event shall showcase how combined efforts of the corporate and academic worlds can optimize the process of building bona fide future leaders for the ever-changing landscape of the Bangladeshi economy, where our future leaders are inspired through “Transition in Career”.This year, the event shall be organized under the tagline- ‘Connecting Talents: Unlock the Border of Opportunities’.

About 200+ reputed companies from 35 industries are expected to take part in this auspicious event. Moreover, this event will effectively open the gateway for companies to meet not only DIU graduates but also its experienced alumni network that is looking forward to making job transitions. We also believe that DIU Job Utsob-2022 will be the perfect platform to find the most suitable candidates for the organizations.

WHO CAN EXHIBIT/SHOWCASE?

  • Employers (private and public) from all sectors (NGOs, Bank, and Financial Institutions, IT/ITES, Multinational companies, Telecom, Local Group of Companies, FMCG, MNC, RMG, Leather, Startup, etc.)
  • Placement agencies
  • Head-hunting agencies
  • Education and training institutions
  • Government and other non-Government organizations.

HIGHLIGHTS OF ‘DIU JOB UTSOB-2022’

The fair will witness participation of highly acclaimed DIU graduates seeking internship and job opportunities.

PARTICIPATION PROCEDURE

The procedures of DIU Job Utsob-2022 follow the following standard pattern and are designed to maximize the payoffs as an outcome of the event.

PARTICIPANTS’ & PARTICIPATIONS DETAILS

The Fair will witness the participation of highly acclaimed DIU Graduates seeking internship and Job opportunities.

DIU Job Utsob-2022 will be graced by the active participation of more than 4000 graduates from batches: 182,183 & 191 of Daffodil International University’s diversified 24 Disciplines and 44 programs under 05 faculties and also- skillful and technology enthusiast alumni seeking potential employment and placement opportunities, whereas the current students of the 9th-11th semester will participate in the event seeking internship opportunities.

Approximately 2500 graduating students and 1500 graduates would be showcasing their industry-ready 21st-century employability skills and competencies through the event.

VISITORS SEGMENTS:

  • Candidates - employed, under-employed or unemployed - with professional qualifications looking for a job
  • Candidates with experience and technical skills
  • New graduates from 24 diversified disciplines and 44 programs under 05 faculties
  • current students of the 9th-11th semester will participate in the event seeking internship opportunities
  • Candidates looking for a second career
  • Candidates in career transition
  • Candidates looking to return to work
  • Candidates attracted to self-employment and entrepreneurship

PROGRAM AND ACTIVITIES:

  • Event Kick-off through Press Conference
  • Grand Inaugural & Closing Ceremony
  • Seminars/Workshop/Career Guideline Sessions
  • Walk in Interview
  • Resume Submissions
  • The Grand Exhibition:

    • The 04 days long exhibition, running in parallel with the other activities, would provide job and employment showcasing opportunities for all industries. The exhibition initiative will provide a distraction-free networking environment to the exhibitors and will assist to become the definitive learning point and business gathering center also.

    The grand exhibition will be held at ‘Helmet’ at Daffodil Smart City (DSC) under the following zones-

    1. Welcoming area
    2. Sponsors area
    3. Exhibitors Area
    4. Walk-in-Interview Area

    Besides these there will be a registration counter and information center at the entrance point of the venue.

Because- DIU Job Utsob-2022 provides a unique opportunity for all stakeholders and it will be the handshaking point of the industry & the academia to assist our graduates to make the transition from campus to corporate.

Because- you will enter a world of mutual benefit.
Employers, trainers, educators, consultants, career services, and government services shall gather to meet the candidates and discover mutual benefits in the labor market.

Because- you will meet the future workforce of your organization.

Because- you will meet the experienced candidates according to your selection criteria.

Because- you will develop the largest network in the shortest time

Because- you will have an extensive and impactful advertising campaign.

And….

  • Promote your services and enhance your branding.
  • Conduct or schedule interviews with preselected candidates.
